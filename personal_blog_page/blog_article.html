Posted on October 24, 2024, by Basel Turkman

"The journey of a thousand miles begins with one step." – Lao Tzu

Starting my journey as a front-end developer has been both challenging and rewarding. From learning the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to mastering frameworks like React, the process has taught me valuable skills. I’ve developed a strong understanding of responsive design, user experience, and how to create visually appealing interfaces. Each project has presented unique challenges that pushed me to think critically and innovate. As I continue to grow, I’m excited to take on new challenges and contribute to meaningful projects in the tech industry.
